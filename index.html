About Us

Proudly black-owned logistics company delivering excellence since 2023

Welcome to Kabo Logistics (Pty) Ltd, your trusted partner in transportation solutions. Founded in 2023, Kabo Logistics is a proudly black-owned logistics company dedicated to delivering excellence in service.

We offer comprehensive Third-party logistics services, extending our expertise to various industries with short and long haul delivery services.

We pride ourselves in providing quality transportation solutions to our customers, ensuring timely, safe and effective delivery service.
